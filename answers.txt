Q1: Why might be consider using git?

Answer: To be able to keep track of any changes we make and revert any bad 
updates

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: It could be applied to biology because in experiments with a lot 
of subjects and variables being tested, data science can be used to easily
parse the data and make conclusions.


Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the 2 branches whereas rebasing takes the commits 
from one branch and re-commits them to the second branch.
